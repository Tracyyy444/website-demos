    OUR HISTORY


    In 2022, our founder Tracy started Lustre & Bloom Haven with a dream: to empower individuals through beauty. Inspired After experiencing a lack of inclusive beauty products, the founder sought to create solutions for all, we opened our first store in Kigali City.

    From those humble beginnings, we have grown from a small studio in a cozy downtown space but now has grown into a thriving multi-location business serving thousands of loyal customers, and today, we proudly serve a diverse clientele from across the city and have gained recognition for our exceptional customer experience and actually We've grown from a team of two passionate beauty experts to a family of 25+ skilled professionals

    Over the years, we have introduced Lashes extension and Press-on Nails , received ABBP (Africa Best Beauty Products), and stayed true to our commitment to eco-friendly practices, using biodegradable packaging and sourcing ingredients ethically and "Giving back is at the heart of what we do, partnering with local organizations to empower women through education.

    Today, we continue to serve our community with a welcoming space where everyone feels beautiful and the mission to value and expand our reach, bringing our unique approach to beauty to more communities., with the vision to transform how people think about beauty, making it a tool for empowerment and self-expression. looking ahead to a brighter, more beautiful future."
